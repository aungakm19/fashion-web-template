About Us

Paris Fashion: A collection of French inspired outfit ideas and French Muses

The story of Paris Fashion began in 1985, at a small shop in the heart of Paris. Admired everywhere, the French fashion and the French style is known in the whole world as a classy and elegant style. You can get some style tips from the men & women to achieve the look when you’re elegant but it seems like you’ve just got the first things in your closet, matched them together and you looked perfect! Tailor is your shopping destination for fashion and clothes. We offer quality at the best price and in a sustainable way.

We work together with famous fashion designers & fabric professionals for choosing the perfect materials and patterns makes our products unique. The choice for the fabric to use for any type of clothing is a very crucial step in clothing production. Fabric and pattern work altogether. The most popular fabric types out there we commonly use are cotton, rayon, denim, silk, satin, linen, wool & flannel. Our luxurious fabrics to design & create the wardrobe of everyone’s dreams.
